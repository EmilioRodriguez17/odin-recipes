Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Chicken fried rice, just like they serve in the restaurants! A stir fry with chicken, rice, soy sauce and veggies like peas, carrots, mushrooms, and bell peppers. This is easy to make and the portions lasts the whole day. Best of all it is delicious !

Ingredients

  • salt
  • pepper
  • chili flakes
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • chicken thighs
  • eggs
  • cooked rice
  • onions
  • mushrooms
  • peas
  • carrots
  • any other vegtable of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook your choice of rice. Leave it in the fridge overnight.
  2. Cut up and season the chicken thighs to your liking.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and saute until soft, then add chicken and 2 tablespoons soy sauce and stir-fry for 5 to 6 minutes.
  4. Stir in carrots, mushrooms , pea pods and any other vegtables and stir-fry another 5 minutes. Then add rice and stir thoroughly.
  5. Finally, stir in scrambled eggs and 1/3 cup soy sauce, heat through and serve hot.
